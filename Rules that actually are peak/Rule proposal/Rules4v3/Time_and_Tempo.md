# Time
Time is spent on most [skills](./Cards_and_Skills.md/#skill) for activation.
Time is kept track of with a personal stack of chips.
The player with the most time has to use a skill or [wait](#wait).
Once everyone has reached 0 time the turn is over.
# Tempo
[skills](./Cards_and_Skills.md/#skill) can give tempo.
At the start of a new turn all tempo is converted into [time](#time).
# Wait
This is an action available to everyone at anytime.
Using the action you can remove any amount of [time](#time) from your stack.
